Ceramic implants are a modern alternative to traditional titanium implants, offering excellent biocompatibility and natural aesthetics. Made from high-quality zirconia, they are strong, durable, and blend seamlessly with natural teeth, making them ideal for patients with metal sensitivities or those seeking a more natural look.

Metal ceramics are made of metals and consist of different types depending on the material used, it contains metal and ceramic. You can ask your dentist about the dental crown that is best for you. In this, metals can be nickel, chromium, titanium, gold and molybdenum. Each has its benefits and limitations. For instance, nickel based metal crowns are highly affordable but can cause allergy, whereas gold crowns are best as they are biocompatible but are highly expensive.

Zirconia implants are a state-of-the-art alternative to traditional titanium implants, known for their excellent biocompatibility and natural aesthetics. Crafted from high-quality zirconia, these implants are strong, durable, and blend seamlessly with natural teeth. They are an ideal choice for patients with metal sensitivities or those seeking a more natural-looking dental restoration.

Nightguard Soft is a comfortable and effective solution for protecting teeth from grinding and clenching during sleep. Made from soft, flexible material, it provides a cushioning effect that reduces the risk of tooth damage and relieves jaw pain. Ideal for those seeking a gentle yet durable night-time dental guard, Nightguard Soft ensures a restful sleep while safeguarding your oral health.

A Hand Retainer is a custom-made appliance designed to maintain the position of teeth after orthodontic treatment. Crafted from durable materials, it ensures your teeth stay in their corrected positions, preventing shifting and maintaining your beautiful smile. Comfortable and easy to wear, Hand Retainers are essential for preserving the results of your orthodontic work.

A denture is a custom-made dental appliance designed to replace missing teeth and restore your smile. Made from durable materials, dentures provide a natural look and comfortable fit, improving your ability to eat and speak. Whether full or partial, dentures help maintain facial structure and enhance overall oral health.
